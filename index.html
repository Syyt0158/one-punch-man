One Punch-Man is a Japanese series that was published online in 2009. After the rising popularity, the series has also been released in manga form from the year 2012 and translated into it English. In addition, an anime has been developed that closely follows the manga series. The first season became released in 2015 and the second in 2019.

Saitama

A famous superhero who, after three years of training, is now able to defeat any enemy in one punch, hence the name "One-Punch Man". Because of his unbelieving powers Saitama it tired of fighting monsters and longs for an opponent who is physically worthy of him.

Genos

A cyborg seeking revenge on another cyborg who murdered his family and destroyed his hometown. Genos volunteers as Saitama's apprentice in hopes of becoming as strong as he is.

The manga remake of One-Punch Man is illustrated by Yusuke Murata. It has been published on Shueisha's Tonari no Young Jump website since June 14, 2012. The chapters are periodically collected and published in tankōbon volumes. The first volume was published on December 12, 2012. A radio drama CD was bundled with the ninth volume released in August 2015. As of November 2, 2023, 29 volumes have been published.

Heroes (ヒーロー達たち, Hīrō Tachi) are individuals who fight for justice against evil in the world. They protect citizens from harm from monsters, villains, natural disasters and other threats. Most heroes are registered with the Hero Association and are divided into four classes of increasing prestige: C-Class, B-Class, A-Class and S-Class. Heroes are given a number ranking within their respective classes.
